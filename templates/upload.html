About Eye Disease Detection

This tool allows you to upload an image of an eye to detect potential eye diseases. The detection process utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze the uploaded image and provide a diagnosis.

Eye diseases can often be detected early through regular check-ups and imaging. Early detection and treatment are crucial for maintaining good eye health and preventing vision loss.
